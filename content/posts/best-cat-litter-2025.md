# Best Cat Litter in 2025: Clumping, Crystal, and Natural Options Compared

Cat litter is not a glamorous topic, but choosing the right one significantly affects your cat's willingness to use the litter box (the #1 cause of inappropriate elimination is litter box dissatisfaction) and your quality of life at home.

## Types of Cat Litter

**Clumping clay:** The most popular type. Forms solid clumps when wet for easy scooping. Provides good odor control. Heavy and dusty.

**Crystal/silica gel:** Absorbs moisture and locks in odor. Less frequent complete changes needed. More expensive per bag but lasts longer.

**Natural/biodegradable:** Made from corn, wheat, walnut shells, wood, or paper. Flushable options available. Better for cats with dust sensitivities.

**Non-clumping clay:** Absorbs but doesn't form clumps. Lower cost but requires full litter box changes more frequently.

**Price:** ~$25/month (subscription) | **Type:** Silica crystal

PrettyLitter is unique: its crystals change color based on pH levels in your cat's urine, potentially indicating urinary tract infections, kidney disease, or bladder stones before symptoms are obvious.

Beyond the health monitoring function, PrettyLitter controls odor extremely well — crystals absorb moisture and eliminate it through evaporation, requiring only solid waste removal and a full monthly change.

**Best for:** Health-conscious cat owners and multi-cat households with any history of urinary issues.

## How Much Litter to Use

**Standard box:** Fill to 3-4 inches depth. Less than 3 inches gives cats insufficient depth to dig and cover — they may refuse to use the box.

**For clumping litter:** The full depth allows clumps to form completely and separate from clean litter, extending time between full changes.

---

## The Number One Rule: Cats Are Picky

The best cat litter is the one your cat will actually use. Cats can reject new litters for reasons that seem arbitrary — texture, scent, particle size. If you switch litters and your cat starts eliminating outside the box, switch back.

If you want to transition litters: mix 25% new with 75% old, then gradually increase the new litter proportion over 2-3 weeks.

## Final Thoughts

Dr. Elsey's Ultra is the most universally accepted clumping litter. PrettyLitter is worth the premium if health monitoring gives you peace of mind. Ökocat is the best natural option.

Whatever litter you choose: scoop daily, do a full change every 2-4 weeks (monthly for crystal), and maintain one litter box per cat plus one extra. Litter box hygiene is the single biggest factor in preventing inappropriate elimination.

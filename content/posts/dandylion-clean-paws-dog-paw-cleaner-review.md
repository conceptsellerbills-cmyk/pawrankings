## Formula: Why No-Rinse Matters

Traditional paw cleaning methods — dunking paws in a water-filled cup washer or running them under the tap — require drying afterward. Wet paw pads left un-dried are prone to **interdigital moisture buildup**, which creates ideal conditions for yeast and bacterial growth, paw licking, and irritation over time.

The Dandylion Clean Paws foam lifts and encapsulates dirt particles, so when you wipe the foam away, the dirt comes with it. Because the formula is designed to be **fully removed by wiping** (not rinsing), there's no water residue left on the paw. The pads are clean and dry after a single towel pass.

The **pH-balanced formula** is important for long-term use. Dog skin has a different pH range than human skin (more neutral, approximately 6.5–7.5 vs. human skin's 4.5–5.5). Cleansers designed for humans — or cheap pet products that don't account for pH — can gradually disrupt the skin's natural barrier with repeated use, leading to dryness, cracking, and increased sensitivity. Dandylion's formula is calibrated for dog skin pH, making it safe for daily use without drying out the paw pads.

The **fragrance-free** formulation is equally important. Many dog paw products use masking fragrances that dogs find stressful (dogs experience scent far more intensely than humans), and synthetic fragrances can trigger allergic reactions in sensitive animals. No fragrance means no irritation and a calmer cleaning experience.
